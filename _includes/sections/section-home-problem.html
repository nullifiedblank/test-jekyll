The Digital Dilemma:

Trying to escape the chaos

Technology was supposed to make our lives better. And in some ways, it does. But at the expense of feeling overwhelmed, drained, and 'buzzing'. And despite promises, it often adds more stress than it alleviates:

  • Stress of Modern Living

    We're severely outgunned: the world’s brightest minds converge around our electronics. The goal? Extract as much of our attention and nervous energy as possible.

  • Tech Overwhelm

    Despite regulatory limits per-device, devices are absolutely everywhere. We feel "plugged in" constantly, and our energy levels experience "death by a thousand cuts".

  • Health Risks of EMF Exposure

    Electromagnetic fields (EMFs) have long carried potential health risks even in small amounts, compromising our clarity and mental state. Yet the number of devices emitting them keeps going up.

Our minds and our focus are under constant EMF attack.
And 'eliminating tech' simply isn’t possible in our modern lives.
It’s no wonder many of us long for a reprieve from the digital chaos…
